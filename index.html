About 

Composer of Acoustic and Electroactic Music

Jeremy Van Buskirk is a composer of a wide range of music including orchestra, chamber music, and electronic music.  He particularly enjoys introducing young musician to new music.

Jeremy has also developed software tools in Max, SuperCollider, and Pyo for both creative and pedogogical use.     

Jeremy teaches composition and theory at the Longy School of Music of Bard College.
